Tell Greg About Yourself
This game is for two players. One player is (X) and has the color blue, the other is (O) and the color is red. The goal is to get three boxes in a row (across, down, or diagonally). When you enter your letter into a box and click OK, the box will change to your color. The game ends when either a player wins, or a tie is made....
